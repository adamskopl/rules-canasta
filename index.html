Overview

  • Players number: 4.
  • Cards: 2 standards decks of cards + 4 Jokers (108 cards total).
  • Target: to get more points than the opposing team after one of the teams gets 5000 points.

Starting the game

  1. Players are divided in two teams (randomly). Players from the same team sit facing each other, having opponents on their sides.
  2. First player is chosen (randomly).

Playing the game

Round setup

Every round starts with a following setup:
  • Each player is dealt 11 cards. Players see their cards and keep them secret (also from their partner).
  • Rest of the cards are placed in the center of a table face down creating a stock.
  • First card is drawn from the stock and placed face up next to it starting a discard pile.

Rounds order

  1. Starting from the first player, every player takes a turn, passing turn to a player on their left. The player turn consists of:
    1. Drawing a top card from the stock or taking the discard pile (mandatory step).
    2. Playing melds (optional step).
    3. Discarding a card from a hand on the top of the discard pile (mandatory step).
  2. Players play their turns until one of the following occurs:
    • One of the players goes out.
    • One of the players wants to or should draw a card from the empty stock.
  3. After playing turns is finished, teams count their scores.
    • If any team scores 5000 points or more, the team with a higher score wins.
    • If both teams score less than 5000 points each, a new round is played.

Types of cards

There are 4 types of cards:

  • Red threes.
  • Black threes.
  • Wild cards: 2s and Jokers.
  • Natural cards: all other cards.

Melds

Melds definition

  • A group of 3 or more cards.
  • All natural cards have to be of a same rank.
  • There has to be at least 2 natural cards.
  • There could be a maximum of 3 wild cards.

Canasta

  • A meld of 7 or more cards is called a Canasta.
  • If all cards in a Canasta are natural, then that Canasta is also natural.
  • If a Canasta contains one or more wild cards, then that Canasta is also wild.
